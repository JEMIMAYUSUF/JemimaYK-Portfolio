The Netflix Stock Price Data Analysis project involves analyzing historical stock prices of Netflix to understand trends, volatility, and key factors influencing stock movements. By examining data such as daily closing prices, trading volumes, and external market events, the project aims to uncover patterns, identify potential predictors of price changes, and explore correlations with industry trends, earnings reports, and broader economic indicators. The analysis could also include time-series forecasting to predict future stock prices and support investment decision-making.

Data Set

The analysis of Netflix's stock price data reveals significant correlations between key events, such as earnings reports and major content releases, with stock price volatility. The data suggests that while subscriber growth and market sentiment play crucial roles in influencing stock performance, external factors like competition and economic trends also contribute to fluctuations.
