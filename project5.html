Diwali Sales (Using Power Bi)

  • Home

The Diwali Sales Dashboard on Power BI provides an interactive visualization of sales performance during the festive season. It showcases key metrics such as total sales, product-wise revenue, regional sales distribution, and customer demographics. The dashboard enables users to drill down into specific categories, compare year-over-year performance, and identify top-selling products. With real-time data updates and intuitive visualizations, this dashboard helps businesses make informed decisions to optimize sales strategies during Diwali, ensuring they maximize profitability during this critical shopping period.

The analysis highlighted key trends that can inform future marketing and inventory decisions, ensuring better preparation for subsequent Diwali seasons. Overall, this project demonstrated the power of data-driven insights in optimizing sales strategies and maximizing profitability during peak shopping periods.
