The HR Dashboard on Power BI offers a comprehensive view of key human resources metrics, providing insights into employee demographics, attendance, performance, and turnover rates. It allows HR teams to track important KPIs such as headcount, employee satisfaction, training effectiveness, and recruitment metrics. The interactive features enable users to filter data by department, location, or role, and drill down into specific areas like retention trends or diversity statistics. This dashboard empowers HR professionals to make data-driven decisions, improve workforce management, and enhance overall organizational efficiency.

The HR Analysis provided critical insights into workforce dynamics, enabling a deeper understanding of employee performance, retention, and recruitment trends. Through the use of comprehensive data visualizations, we identified areas for improvement in talent management, such as reducing turnover rates and enhancing training programs. The analysis also highlighted key factors driving employee satisfaction and productivity, offering actionable recommendations for HR strategies. Overall, this project underscored the value of data-driven approaches in optimizing human resource management and aligning workforce initiatives with organizational goals.
